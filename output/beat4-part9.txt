<h2>Part 7: Combat Encounter — Dead on the Mountain Road</h2>

<p><em>Approximately Sólmánuður 12th. Night. The party camps on the mountain road between the toll and the Stair.</em></p>

<h3>Context</h3>

<p>The "tracks" that Warden-Sergeant Eirik warned about belong to a pair of <strong>Frost-Draugr</strong>—dead things that have wandered up from the lowland barrows, drawn by some instinct toward the heights. They are not scouts or soldiers. They are the mindless dead, following a pull they don’t understand—southwest, always southwest, toward Hrafnborg. The party’s campfire draws them off-course.</p>
