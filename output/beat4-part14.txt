rong> Moderate (EV 21 for 4 heroes)</p>

<p><strong>Composition:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>1 Goblin Monarch (Leader, EV 12)</li>
<li>2 Goblin Warriors (Horde Harrier, EV 3 each)</li>
<li>4 Goblin Runners (Minion Harrier, EV 3 for the squad)</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Terrain:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Barricade:</strong> Provides three-quarters cover. Can be dismantled (easy Might test) or climbed over (easy Agility test, costs 2 extra movement).</li>
<li><strong>Cliff Walls:</strong> The goblins start on the walls above (Climb movement). 3 squares up. Creatures without Climb must make a easy Might test to scale.</li>
<li><strong>Scree Slope:</strong> Difficult terrain. A creature pushed through scree must make an easy Agility test or fall prone.</li>
<li><strong>Narrow Path:</strong> Only 3 squares wide between the cliff face and the drop. Limits flanking opportunities for larger creatures.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Tactics:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>The Goblin Runners charge from the cliff walls, exploiting Crafty to avoid opportunity attacks.</li>
<li>The Goblin Warriors use Spear Charge for hit-and-run tactics, retreating to high ground.</li>
<li>The Goblin Monarch hangs back on the boulder, using Handaxe on two targets at range and <em>Get in Here!</em> to summon more Runners.</li>
<li>If a PC is isolated, the Monarch uses Meat Shield to redirect attacks to nearby Runners.</li>
<li>When pressed, the Monarch triggers <em>Kill!</em> (VA3) to punish clustering.</li>
</ul>

<h4>Goblin Malice Features</h4>

<ul>
<li><strong>Goblin Mode</strong> (3 Malice): Each goblin shifts up to 3 squares. Until end of round, goblins have double edge on power rolls against creatures larger than them.</li>
<li><strong>Tiny Stabs</strong> (5 Malice): 10 burst. Each enemy in the area takes 3 damage for each goblin adjacent to them.</li>
<li><strong>Swamp Stink</strong> (7 Malice): 10 burst. Each enemy must succeed on an easy Intuition test or become dazed (save ends). Each goblin then shifts up to 3 squares.</li>
</ul>
