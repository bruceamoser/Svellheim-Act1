<h3>Round 1 Interlude: The Extinguished Shrine</h3>
<div style="background:#2a2a3a; color:#e0d8c8; padding:14px 18px; margin:12px 0; border-radius:6px; border-left:4px solid #c4a03c; font-style:italic;">
<p>By the roadside, a cairn of stacked stones stands waist-high. Its centre is hollow—a bowl-shaped depression where a flame should burn. The ash is cold. Dead wildflowers, frost-brittle, lie scattered around the base. Someone left offerings. No one came to tend the fire.</p>

<p>The cairn’s keystone is carved with a single runic word. It reads: <em>Remember.</em></p>
</div>

<p>A moment for the PCs to reflect on what the Ever-Embers mean. If anyone makes an offering (even just a breath warmed into their hands and placed in the basin), the cairn’s keystone warms—just for a moment—then goes cold again. Yggdrasil is listening, but too weak to answer.</p>
