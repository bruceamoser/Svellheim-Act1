<h3>Round 3 Interlude: The Watched Feeling</h3>
<div style="background:#2a2a3a; color:#e0d8c8; padding:14px 18px; margin:12px 0; border-radius:6px; border-left:4px solid #c4a03c; font-style:italic;">
<p>As you crest a hill, a feeling settles over the group—a prickling at the back of the neck. Not a sound. Not a movement. Just the unmistakable sense that <strong>something</strong> out there knows where you are.</p>

<p>The ridge to the northwest is empty. The sky is grey. Nothing moves.</p>

<p>But the feeling doesn’t leave.</p>
</div>

<p>This is the first effect of Grafvitnir’s network. Intelligence from Hrafnborg has reached agents in the field, and the PCs are now being tracked—not by the hound, but by the Pale Maw’s informant chains: corrupted couriers, compromised Chainwarden posts, and Wyrd-touched carrion birds with silver eyes circling high above.</p>

<p>If anyone uses magic to scan: nothing registers. Grafvitnir’s agents are mortal. The Wyrd-channels that feed them are invisible to arcane detection.</p>
