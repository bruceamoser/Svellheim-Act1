<h4>Encounter Setup</h4>

<p><strong>Encounter Difficulty:</strong> Moderate (EV 21 for 4 heroes)<br>
<strong>Hard Variant:</strong> Add Warden-Captain Halvard (EV 33 total)</p>

<p><strong>Standard Composition:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>1 Chainwarden Enforcer (Platoon Defender)</li>
<li>1 Chainwarden Artillerist (Platoon Artillery)</li>
<li>2 Chainwarden Militia (Horde Brute)</li>
<li>4 Chainwarden Guards (Minion Brute)</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Hard Composition:</strong> Add Warden-Captain Halvard (Leader)</p>

<p><strong>Terrain:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Stone Bridge:</strong> 2 squares wide, 6 squares long. Railings provide half cover.</li>
<li><strong>Frozen Ravine:</strong> 3 squares deep. Falling deals 10 damage. Climbing out requires a easy Might test.</li>
<li><strong>Tree Line:</strong> The artillerist starts in the trees 10 squares back from the bridge, providing three-quarters cover.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Tactics:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>The Enforcer holds the bridge chokepoint, taunting the first PC across.</li>
<li>The Artillerist fires from the tree line, exploiting their range and cover-ignoring shots.</li>
<li>The Militia flank around the ravine edges to hit squishier targets.</li>
<li>The Guards swarm whoever crosses the bridge.</li>
<li>If Halvard is present, he uses <strong>Warden's Warrant</strong> to mark the most dangerous PC, then sweeps with <strong>Chain-Flail Sweep</strong> when the party clusters.</li>
</ul>

<h4>Human Malice Features</h4>

<p>These Chainwardens use the <strong>Human</strong> keyword and share the following Malice features:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Exploit Opening</strong> (3 Malice): Each Chainwarden gains an edge on the next ability they use this round. If the target has a condition, the edge is a double edge instead.</li>
<li><strong>Alchemical Device</strong> (5 Malice): 3 burst within 10. Each non-human creature in the area takes 10 corruption damage.</li>
<li><strong>Staying Power</strong> (7 Malice): Each non-minion Chainwarden regains Stamina equal to 5 × their level.</li>
</ul>
