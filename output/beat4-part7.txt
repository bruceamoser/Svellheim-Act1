<h2>Part 4: Point of Interest — The Hollow Waystation</h2>

<p>Between the first montage and the Chainwarden Toll, the party encounters this location. It can serve as a rest stop or a trap.</p>

<div style="background:#2a2a3a; color:#e0d8c8; padding:14px 18px; margin:12px 0; border-radius:6px; border-left:4px solid #c4a03c; font-style:italic;">
<p>The road widens at a stone-built waystation—one of the old Embertender rest-houses that once dotted the major routes. The roof is intact. The walls are solid. Smoke rises from the chimney.</p>

<p>Someone is inside.</p>
</div>

<p>Inside: <strong>Bryn Ketilsdottir</strong>, a former Embertender who abandoned her post when her settlement’s Ever-Ember went out. She’s been living in the waystation for three weeks, burning furniture for warmth, rationing a dwindling supply of dried fish.</p>

<ul>
<li>
<p>Bryn is not hostile, but she is deeply ashamed. She failed her oath. The Ember she tended flickered and died in the night—no attack, no catastrophe, it just…​ stopped. She couldn’t bear to face her community.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>If the PCs offer compassion: Bryn shares what she knows. The Ever-Embers are going out <strong>without external cause</strong>. Three more have failed since hers—she heard the news from refugees on the road. "It’s not the cold killing them," she says. "It’s something underneath. The ground is sick."</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>If the PCs push her for information (easy Presence test): Bryn describes the night her Ember died. "I heard a sound. Not from outside—from <strong>below</strong>. Like something exhaling. The flame bent toward the floor and went out. The stone was warm for an hour afterward, as if the fire had sunk into the earth."</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>If the PCs share food: Bryn is overwhelmed. She insists on giving them her last useful possession—a <strong>Chainwarden toll-token</strong> (worth one free passage through a checkpoint, saving Heat or Word at the Greypass Toll).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>If the PCs help Bryn secure the waystation (or if they show genuine kindness), she also offers a battered iron lantern from the waystation&rsquo;s stores: the @UUID[Item.SVH00WayLantrn01]{Hollow Waystation Lantern}. &ldquo;It was the Embertender&rsquo;s before mine. The flame won&rsquo;t die in the wind. Take it &mdash; I don&rsquo;t deserve to carry their light.&rdquo;</p>
</li>
</ul>
