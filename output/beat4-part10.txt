<h3>Terrain Features</h3>

<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Campfire:</strong> Provides light in a 4-square radius. Any undead that starts its turn within 2 squares of the fire takes 3 fire damage. The PCs' fire is both weapon and vulnerability—it draws the dead, but it also hurts them.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Collapsed Stone Walls:</strong> Provide half cover and block movement. Can be toppled (easy Might test) to create difficult terrain.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Scree Slope:</strong> The hillside above the camp is loose stone. A creature pushed into the scree must make an easy Agility test or fall prone and slide 2 squares downhill.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Darkness Beyond Firelight:</strong> Creatures outside the campfire’s light radius have concealment. The wisps exploit this—they hover at the edge of visibility.</p>
</li>
</ul>
